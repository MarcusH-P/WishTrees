ABOUT OUR MISSION

What we stand for:


Our main goal is to combat the UN Goal 15: Life on land. We represent those who live on our land but don't have their own voice and therefore cannot do anything to help themselves. Our website primarily focuses on protecting, restoring, and managing forests to stop animals suffering from the loss of their homes. Our fight against deforestation cannot be done without you, so we need your help. Please support us by donating today! The foundation for our life on this planet depends on a flourishing life on land.







How you can help


You can help Wish Trees achieve its goals by donating money through playing games and buying our merchandise. We are partners with multiple charities, who have joined with us to raise awareness and share their stories, to encourage you to make a change! Donating, however, is not the only way you can make a difference. Do your bit for your community by reducing the amount of paper you use by avoiding printing and using electronic correspondence instead, start composting or rally your friends and help to clean up local forests and parks.The earth needs your help!
